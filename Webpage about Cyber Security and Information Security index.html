What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity focuses on safeguarding digital systems, networks, and data from cyber threats such as hacking, malware, ransomware, and phishing. It involves practices and technologies designed to protect online infrastructure from unauthorized access, disruption, or destruction.

Key Components:

  • Network Security: Protects internal networks from unauthorized access.
  • Application Security: Ensures software and devices are free from vulnerabilities.
  • Data Security: Protects sensitive information from breaches and leaks.

Cybersecurity is essential in safeguarding personal data, financial systems, and critical infrastructure in a digital age dominated by cyber threats.
